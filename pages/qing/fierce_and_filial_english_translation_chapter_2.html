Q&A

Is it really free?
Yes. You’ll be able to read the entire book (one chapter at a time) and you’ll never be asekd to pay anything.

Why are you giving this away for free?
A book is meant to be read. I’d rather have a lot of people reading this book than make a lot of money. I do intend to sell copies at some point... but everyone who believes in this project before the book is complete gets the book for free. (Plus, I’m hoping that everyone who read the book for free will help me promote it and write reviews once I start selling copies. But if you just want to read without helping out, that’s cool too.)

If it’s free, why are you requiring people to sign up?
Once the translation has been completed, edited, and published, I’ll remove the free chapters and start selling copies of the book. I would be willing to post these chapters without requiring signup... except that everything made public on the Internet can ever be taken down.

Can I distribute the chapters?
You can share chapter 1 and the preface. But please don’t share any of the other chapters or post them online.
