Privacy Policy

We will not collect any personal data about you or track you unless you explicitly request us to do so.

If you would like us to track you, please click here.

Why would I want to volunteer to be tracked?

Tracking visitors helps us better understand which pages are the most popular. Without tracking visitors, it's hard to discern the difference between 100 people viewing a page and a single person hitting refresh 100 times. But we're not going to track you without your explicit request. It's totally up to you.
